WWF

The World Wide Fund for Nature(WWF) is an international organization working on issues regarding the convservation, research and restoration of the environment, formaly named the world wildlife Fund .WWF was founded in 1961.

WWF Panda symbol

The Panda has become the symbol of WWF. The well-known panda logo of WWF oiginated from a panda named chi chi that was transeferred from the Beijing Zoo to the London Zoo in the same year of the establisment of WWF.
